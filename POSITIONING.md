# WAIMS-GM Positioning

## What It Is

WAIMS-GM is the basketball decision-support product in the WAIMS ecosystem.

It is designed for a GM, coach, recruiting staffer, portal staffer, or basketball ops lead who needs to turn player information and roster context into a faster, more explainable acquisition decision.

The product workflow is:

1. Create or load a player file
2. Evaluate it against current team context
3. Compare options on a board
4. Leave with a meeting-ready dossier or comparison brief

WAIMS-GM is not positioned as a black-box AI GM. It is a structured basketball workflow for scoring, comparing, and defending roster decisions.

## What It Produces

WAIMS-GM produces:

- recommendation and overall score
- component-level score breakdown
- contextual decision lens
- tension points and assumptions
- compare verdicts and decision snapshot
- exportable dossier and comparison brief

The goal is not abstract analytics. The goal is a usable staff artifact for meetings and roster decisions.

## Strongest Commercial Wedge

The strongest near-term wedge is lower-resource college basketball:

- NCAA D2
- NAIA
- JUCO
- smaller-staff college programs

This is the cleanest starting market because the pain point is real, the staffing gap is obvious, and the ROI story is easier to explain than it is for pro or high-major programs with larger internal resources.

## WAIMS-GM vs WAIMS Python

WAIMS Python should be framed as the broader WAIMS platform or intelligence layer.

WAIMS-GM should be framed as the basketball-specific application built on top of that foundation.

Use this split:

- WAIMS Python = platform, shared logic, broader intelligence layer
- WAIMS-GM = front-office basketball workflow for evaluation, comparison, and decision support

## When It Should Stay Separate

WAIMS-GM should stay separate when:

- the buyer is basketball-specific
- the workflow is front-office or coaching-specific
- the UI and demo motion are basketball-specific
- the proof of value depends on roster, portal, or recruiting decisions

## When It Could Become a WAIMS Module

WAIMS-GM could become a WAIMS module later when:

- identity and auth are shared across products
- reporting and exports should live in one platform shell
- evaluation infrastructure is shared across multiple WAIMS products
- the business benefits more from one integrated platform than from a focused stand-alone wedge

## Short Positioning Lines

- For coaches: "WAIMS-GM helps your staff move from scattered notes to a clear, meeting-ready player decision with context, tradeoffs, and a usable dossier."
- For GMs: "WAIMS-GM is not a black-box AI GM; it is a structured basketball decision-support workflow for ranking, comparing, and defending acquisition decisions."
- For investors: "WAIMS Python is the platform layer, but WAIMS-GM is the wedge product: a focused, sellable basketball application with a clear initial market in lower-resource college programs."
