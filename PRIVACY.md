# WAIMS-GM Privacy Boundary

WAIMS-GM is a front-office decision-support workspace, not a medical record system.

## What belongs in WAIMS-GM

- public injury or missed-time history
- public reporting and official school availability notes
- video links and movement observations from public film
- advisory diligence summaries for roster pricing and board-stage decisions

## What does not belong in WAIMS-GM

- protected student medical records
- treatment notes, diagnoses, imaging, prescriptions, or rehab plans
- private internal athlete-monitoring exports from WAIMS Python
- anything that would exceed a collaborator's legitimate educational interest

## Product rule

`Med Diligence` in WAIMS-GM is advisory only. It should inform pricing, prioritization, and risk framing for outside prospects. It must not be presented as medical clearance.

## Access rule

Sport science and medical collaborators in WAIMS-GM should see only the prospect files they need to review and should contribute only to public-file diligence, movement observations, and advisory notes.
